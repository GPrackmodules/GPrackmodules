# GP Plugin for VCV Rack 2 by GPaudio

**GP** is a suite of miscellaneous modules for the virtual **VCV Rack 2** modular synthesizer

<h2>Modules</h2>

The following modules are included:

![Rotary Module](Rotary.png "The Rotary Module") &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;![Rotary Module](Rotary-dark.png "The Rotary Module")<br>
[Rotary](Rotary.html) – An emulation of the famous rotating speaker cabinets. 
<br><br>

![Stereo Chorus Module](StereoChorus.png "The Stereo Chorus Module") &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;![Stereo Chorus Module](StereoChorus-dark.png "The Stereo Chorus Module")<br>
[Stereo Chorus](StereoChorus.html) – A chorus effect with up to four stereo voices with external control and modulation through CV inputs
<br><br>
![Chained Mixer Modules](ChainedMixer.png "Chained Mixer With Four Input Channel Modules")  &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;![Chained Mixer Modules](ChainedMixer-dark.png "Chained Mixer With Two Input Channel Modules")<br>
[Chained Mixer](ChainedMixer.html) – Modules for a modular stereo mixer, including Input channels, a Master section and an Aux section
<br><br> 
![AB4 Module](AB4.png "The AB4 Module") &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;![AB4 Module](AB4-dark.png "The AB4 Module") &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;
![AB8 Module](AB8.png "The AB8 Module") &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;![AB8 Module](AB8-dark.png "The AB8 Module")<br>
[AB4](AB4.html) and [AB8](AB8.html) – 4 and 8 channel AB switchers for audio and control voltages
